Yorder - Quick Commerce Platform

Backend contributions to rapid delivery application

Production Node.js Express MySQL Razorpay

Overview

Quick commerce platform for grocery and essentials delivery. Built monetization features including subscription model, referral system, and coupon engine to drive user acquisition and revenue.

My Contributions

  • Subscription Model: Premium membership with recurring billing, grace periods, and benefit tracking (free delivery, exclusive deals)
  • Referral System: User acquisition through referral rewards with milestone bonuses and fraud prevention
  • Coupon Engine: Flexible discount validation with expiry checks, usage limits, and minimum order requirements
  • Automated Renewals: Cron job for subscription renewals with retry logic and email notifications
